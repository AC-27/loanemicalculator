1. Walnut

2. Money Manager Expense & Budget

Best for: Manual Budget Planning

Why Students Love It: Ideal for detail-oriented students who want full visibility of every rupee spent.

3. Goodbudget

Best for: Envelope-Style Budgeting

  • Divides income into categories like Rent, Food, and Leisure
  • Supports multiple devices and cloud sync
  • Manual control encourages disciplined budgeting

Why Students Love It: Encourages forward planning and disciplined expense management.

Why Students Love It: Ends confusion after group trips or dinners. Keeps everything transparent!
