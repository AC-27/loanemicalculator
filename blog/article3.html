PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana: Empowering Indian Households with Free Solar Electricity

The PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana is a transformative initiative by the Government of India, launched on February 15, 2024, aiming to provide sustainable and free electricity to households through rooftop solar panel installations. This scheme not only promotes renewable energy but also offers significant financial savings to citizens.

Key Features of the Scheme

  • Objective: To install rooftop solar panels in 1 crore households across India, providing up to 300 units of free electricity per month.
  • Subsidy: Government offers substantial subsidies directly credited to beneficiaries' bank accounts to cover installation costs.
  • Loan Facility: Collateral-free loans up to ₹2 lakh at a marginal interest rate of 6.75% are available through 12 public sector banks (Source: Economic Times).
  • Implementation: Managed by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy with support from state DISCOMs.

Subsidy Structure

Based on the capacity of the solar installation, the subsidy details are as follows:

  • Up to 2 kW: ₹30,000 per kW (Total ₹60,000)
  • 2 kW to 3 kW: ₹30,000 for first 2 kW and ₹18,000 for the additional kW (Total ₹78,000)
  • Above 3 kW: Maximum subsidy capped at ₹78,000

Note: Subsidies are directly transferred to the beneficiary's bank account post-installation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • Should own a residential property with a suitable rooftop for solar panel installation.
  • Must have a valid electricity connection.
  • Should not have availed any other solar panel subsidy from the government.

Application Process

  1. Visit the official portal: https://pmsuryaghar.gov.in/
  2. Register by selecting your state and electricity distribution company (DISCOM).
  3. Submit necessary documents: proof of identity, address, electricity bill, and property ownership.
  4. Choose a registered vendor for installation.
  5. Post-installation, apply for net metering through your DISCOM.
  6. Upon verification, the subsidy amount will be credited to your bank account within 30 days.

Benefits of the Scheme

  • Reduction in monthly electricity bills.
  • Promotion of clean and sustainable energy.
  • Decrease in carbon footprint and environmental conservation.
  • Creation of employment opportunities in the renewable energy sector.
  • Support for India's commitment to achieving 500 GW of renewable energy capacity by 2030 (Source: LinkedIn Article).
