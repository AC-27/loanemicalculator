Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EMI Calculator?

It is a tool that helps you calculate the monthly installment amount for a loan based on the principal, interest rate, and tenure.

Can I use this for different types of loans?

Yes! You can use this EMI calculator for home loans, car loans, education loans, and personal loans.

What is a Prepayment Calculator?

It shows how making early payments on your loan can reduce the total interest paid and the loan tenure.

Is my data saved or tracked?

No. This calculator runs entirely in your browser. It does not store any personal information or loan data.

Can I share my EMI details?

Yes! You can download a PDF of your EMI chart or share it via WhatsApp or Email directly from the tool.

Is this tool mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! The EMI Calculator is designed to work perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
