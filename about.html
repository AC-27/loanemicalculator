About This EMI Calculator

Our EMI Calculator is a simple and efficient tool designed to help you estimate your monthly loan repayments. Whether you're planning to take out a home loan, car loan, or personal loan, this tool gives you clarity and control over your financial decisions.

Features include:

  • EMI Calculation with visual breakdowns
  • Prepayment calculator to show interest savings
  • PDF download for sharing or printing
  • Dark mode for a pleasant viewing experience

Developed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it's fully responsive and mobile-friendly. No personal data is collected — it’s a privacy-friendly, ad-free tool that you can rely on.

We're constantly working on adding new features and improvements. Stay tuned!
